Algorithm for making a random number guessing game
Steps:

1.Start.

2.Computer generates a secret random number.

3.Take a guess number from the user.

4.If guess is equal to secret number, print "You Win!" and finish.

5.If guess is higher, print "Too High" and try again.

6.If guess is lower, print "Too Low" and try again.

7.End.

Algorithm for finding Max/Min 
Steps:

1.Start.

2.Take a list of numbers.

3.Set the first number as both Max and Min.

4.Compare Max and Min with the next numbers one by one.

5.If a number is bigger, change Max. If smaller, change Min.

6.Print Max and Min.

7.End.
