             <------------1. An Algorithm for making a random number guessing game------------>
Steps:

1.Start.

2.Computer generates a secret random number.(কোড এ থাকবে)

3.Take a guess number from the user.

4.If guess is equal to secret number or the random number, print "You Win!" and finish.

5.If guess is higher, print "The num isToo High" and try again.

6.If guess is lower, print "The numToo Low" and try again.

7.End.

             <----------2. Algorithm for finding Max number(biggest) or Min number(smallest)----------> 
Steps:

1.Start.

2.Take a list of numbers.

3.Set the first number as both Max and Min.

4.Compare Max and Min with the next numbers one by one.

5.If a number is bigger, change Max. If smaller, change Min.

6.Print Max and Min.

7.End.
